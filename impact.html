Our Journey

How we got here

Q1

ReEnvision Founded — January 2024

A small group of students launched ReEnvision after seeing firsthand how limited AI education was for underserved communities.

Q2

First Workshop Series — March 2024

We launched our inaugural "AI for Everyone" workshop, reaching 40 students across two community centers in its first month.

Q3

10 Partnerships Reached — July 2024

By mid-year, we had formed partnerships with 10 local nonprofits and schools, doubling our capacity almost overnight.

Q4

150+ Contributors & 250+ Hours of Content — 2024

Our community surpassed 150 active contributors and our library grew to over 250 hours of freely available curriculum.
